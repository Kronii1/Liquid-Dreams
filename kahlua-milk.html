Kahlua Milk

A cocktail based on liqueur . Literally milk in coffee It is a mixture of Kahlua , a liqueur , and is sweet and soft, and the alcohol content is similar to that of beer . Of course, the alcohol content varies depending on the amount of milk mixed. Kahlua's sweet coffee aroma and milk's mild taste make it popular with people who are not familiar with alcohol.

Ingredients 1 oz kahlua 3~4 oz milk Ice
How to make 1. In an ice-filled old-fashioned glass, pour 2 ounces coffee liqueur. 2. Fill with 2 to 3 ounces cream or milk. Serve and enjoy.
