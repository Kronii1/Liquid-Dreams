David Martinez

The David Martinez is a top-quality alcoholic beverage. The drink was named after David Martinez. Despite being named after David, he did not like carbonated drinks, making the addition of NiCola to the drink something he would not enjoy.

Ingredients Fill glass with ice 1/2 oz of simple syrup 1 1/2 oz of vodka Top with blueberry marble soda (Hata Ramune brand) Add lemon peel
