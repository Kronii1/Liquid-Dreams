Blue Sky

This is a vodka- based cocktail , and belongs to the shooter category. There are many variations of this cocktail, but what they have in common is that they all use blue curacao . Park Jae -woo of Korea was first introduced as the winner of Beefeather Bartender Championship in London, England in 1997.

Ingredients 1/3 oz vodka 1/3 oz blue curacao 1/3 oz peach tree 2 dashes milk
How to make 1. After pouring the peach tree, float in the order of vodka and blue curacao 2. Add a drop or two of milk or Baileys to make a cloud-shaped garnish. 3. Pour it into your mouth and enjoy the taste as it mixes in your mouth.
